Systems Analysis and Design Processes

Introduction:

Systems Analysis and Design, is a step-by-step procedure to develop and maintain a robust information system. No two organizations are exactly the same and therefore the role of a system analyst is to study the existing organizational setup, document organizational needs, provide suggestions and acts as a translator between business and technical units. Thus, a systems analyst is the "marmalade between the management and technical slices" to put in a rather crude sort of way
